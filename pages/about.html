Bio

Born and grew up in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

I obtained my degree at the Faculty of Computer Engineering from Bogura Polytechnic Institute. I am a self thought graphic designer and trying to develop more design skills everyday. I'm very passionate and dedicated to my work. With 3 years of working experience as freelance graphic designer, I have acquired the skills and knowledge necessary to make any design project a success.

A quote I always follow -

Try and leave this world a little better than you found it.Robert Baden-Powell

2016 the year when I received the president's scout award from the president of Bangladesh. It was a wondrous journey, from being a nobody to becoming a president's scout. Learning new things, helping people, and sharing knowledge became my habit. Then graphic design came into my life. Now I'm trying to design the future.
